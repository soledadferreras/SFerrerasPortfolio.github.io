About Me

My journey into data started in the world of scientific research, where I spent over a decade working with complex datasets and leading analytical projects. In that environment, I developed a rigorous and structured approach to data, along with a strong attention to detail and quality. Along the way, I realized that what I enjoy most is not just analyzing data, but making it accessible and useful for others. I’ve worked extensively on reports, presentations, and dashboards, always aiming to translate complex information into clear insights that support both technical and non-technical audiences. Now, I’m focusing on applying my experience in a business context, where data can directly drive decisions and improve processes. I’m currently strengthening my skills in SQL, data modeling, and business analytics, while exploring ways to automate workflows and increase efficiency. I’m a curious and adaptable professional who enjoys learning, collaborating with others, and using data to solve real-world problems.
